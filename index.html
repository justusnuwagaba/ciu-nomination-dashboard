⚙ Connect Data Source
Link your Google Sheet so nominations appear live as they are submitted.

1. Open your Google Sheet → File → Share → Publish to web
2. Choose your sheet tab → Format: CSV → click Publish
3. Copy the published URL and paste below
4. Sheet columns must match: B=Timestamp, C=NomFirst, D=NomLast, H=Position, I=NeeFirst, J=NeeLast

URL should end with pub?output=csv
